Startup Profile — Consilience Bio

Extracted from investment memo, call transcripts (Apr 7 & Apr 8, 2026), and company website. This profile is the single source of truth for mentor scoring.

Sector & Sub-sector

AI-native data infrastructure for multimodal biology (multi-omics). Computational biology SaaS. Life Sciences / BioTech.

Stage & Round

Pre-revenue seed. Raising €2.5M. BPI France Digital Venture III signed term sheet as lead (€1M). ~50% committed. Target close: May–June 2026.

Business Model

B2B SaaS. ~$75K ACV initial. API/SDK/CLI integration (no data migration required). Annual subscription. Projected 90% renewal rate.

Value Proposition

Replace €80–130K consulting engagements with a $75K SaaS license. Compress months of specialist work into hours. 4–6× ROI narrative for biotech/pharma procurement teams.

Technology

3 pillars: Harmonize (LLM-driven metadata & ontology normalization), Integrate (AI-agent orchestrated multi-omics pipelines), Augment (synthetic data generation via digital twin models — POC stage, no direct competitor).

Target Customers

Primary: US biotech/techbio (Boston + California clusters, 1,200 addressable). Secondary: European cosmetics/food/agro corporates (L'Oréal, Nestlé, Lesaffre, Lallemand). Tertiary: Pharma R&D teams.

Team

Hocine Lourdani (CEO, PharmD/MBA, Silicon Valley AI product); Marc Chadeau-Hyam (CSO, Imperial College London, world-leading omics ML); Sébastien Marque (CBO, 3 exits incl. 2× IQVIA, 25 yrs enterprise sales); Mario Cornejo (CTO, 2× founder); Pascale Rondeau (CCO, former Danone).

Geographic Focus

50% US (Boston, SF/San Diego), 25% Europe (France, Germany, UK), 25% Asia (not near-term priority). Sébastien based US with French visa; Hocine between Paris & SF; team hub in Paris.

Key Milestones

MVP launch H2 2026; $500K ARR 2026; $2M+ ARR 2027; Series A (€17M+) H1 2028. Design partners: L'Oréal, UCSF, Santa Ana Bio, Lesaffre, Lallemand. Published: Nature Scientific Reports, SCiX 2025.
